ABOUT

Feel Fine® started off as a project of the Squad 49 for the KickOff (1st step of the Porto Digital Residency Program) and was among the 13 best ideas presented in 2023.

Since Emotional Intelligence is a fundamental skill for any existing human, our mission is to make it possible and affordable for young children.
